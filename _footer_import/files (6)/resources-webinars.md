## Section 03 — Session Format Guide

### What to Expect

**Standard Webinar (45–75 minutes)**  
A structured presentation followed by a moderated Q&A. Questions are collected throughout the session and addressed at the end. All registered attendees receive a recording and any referenced resources within 48 hours.

**Technical Workshop (75–120 minutes)**  
A hands-on session requiring active participation in a guided environment. Workshops are limited to a small number of participants. Sandbox or active deployment access is typically required. Recordings are made available but the interactive elements are only available to live participants.

**Ask Me Anything (60 minutes)**  
An open Q&A session with the Antarious team — no set agenda, no presentation. Bring your questions. These sessions run quarterly and cover whatever participants need. Topics from previous AMA sessions are published on this page.
